Explore Bihar's Fascinating Circuits.

Most Popular Circuits

Buddhist Circuit

The Buddhist Circuit is a trail of the sacred footsteps of Lord Buddha and the important places of his life and teachings across Bihar.

Eco Circuit

Biharâ€™s rich natural scenery and wildlife make it an important destination for ecological tourism. Whether it is getting a glimpse of the magnificent big cats or observing the natural wonders in Bihar, the Eco circuit covers it all.

Jain Circuit

Bihar is a holy place for the Jains as the last Tirthankara of the Jains was born here. A visit to some of the Jain pilgrim destinations in Bihar offers a view into the life and journey of Lord Mahavira.

Ramayan Circuit

Many of the locations mentioned in this legend are in Bihar and considered holy sites for Hindus. Temples depicting the tales of Ram and Sita can be found in this circuit.

Sikh Circuit

A tour of the Sikh Circuit of Bihar will take you through other gurudwaras and Sikh shrines commemorating the events in the life of the Sikh Guru.

Sufi Circuit

During the medieval period, the spiritualistic aura enjoyed by Pataliputra (Patna) attracted many Sufi saints who preached in a very humanistic way that made the region a hub of pilgrimage for the devotees of all religions.
